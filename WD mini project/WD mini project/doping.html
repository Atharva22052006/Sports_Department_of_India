NDTL promotes Clean Sports through High Scientific Standards of Dope Testing

The National Dope Testing Laboratory (NDTL) is an autonomous body of the Government of India. This is a premier analytical testing & research organization established in the country and mandated for dope testing of sportspersons at both-domestic as well as international levels. It is accredited by the World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA) and also accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Testing & Calibration Laboratories (NABL) as per ISO/IEC 17025:2017 for chemical and biological testing.

NDTL is one of the modern & state-of-the-art Laboratories equipped with highly sophisticated analytical equipment and instruments required for human dope testing. It is the only Laboratory of its kind in the country with the most advanced facilities and methods required for Anti-Doping science and allied research, while strictly adhering to the protocols of WADA International Standards for Laboratories (ISLs).

Presently, it is one of the thirty-two (32) WADA accredited Laboratories in the World. It abides by the WADA guidelines and is subject to periodical WADA assessment/review. Consistently striving for excellence, NDTL regularly updates its equipment and enhances the skill of its analytical personnel. The Laboratory is equipped with highly sophisticated instruments like Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS/MS), Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS/MS), and Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry (IRMS), etc. essentially required for testing of urine & blood samples for human sports.

In an effort to realize India’s longstanding potential for achieving self-sustenance in every field and keeping in tune with Government’s ‘Atmanirbhar Bharat’, NDTL has taken up several initiatives of collaborative research in the field of anti-doping science with Premier Scientific Research institutes of India, having signed Memoranda of understanding (MoUs) with National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research (NIPER-G), Guwahati and CSIR-Indian Institute of Integrative Medicine, Jammu (IIIM-J).

Consistently striving for excellence, NDTL regularly updates its equipment and enhances the skill of its analytical personnel. At the very core of NDTL are its scientists, who work tirelessly on research and development of new analytical methods, and ensure that India sets high standards in Dope Science Research. In the process, NDTL shares its expertise with a number of countries and extends dope testing services for over a dozen countries in Asia and beyond.

NDTL is participating in various External Quality Assurance Schemes (EQAS) like World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA), World Association of Anti-Doping Scientists (WAADS) and Swiss Centre for Quality Control (CSCQ). NDTL shares its expertise with many countries and extends dope testing services for over a dozen countries in Asia and beyond. NDTL endeavours to deliver its services appropriate to the client's needs. It strives to continuously improve its services and maintain its quality performances. NDTL aims at systemically promoting skills and knowledge of all its staff members by imparting appropriate training of the highest standards.
