Overview

The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports functioned under the overall guidance of the Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Youth Affairs & Sports during the year. In April 2008, two separate Departments, namely, Department of Youth Affairs and Department of Sports, were created under the Ministry, each Department under the charge of a Secretary to the Government of India. The Department of Sports is a government entity dedicated to promoting sports, fitness, and wellness among citizens. Established with the vision of fostering a culture of physical activity and teamwork, the department plays a crucial role in shaping the future of sports in the nation.

Our Mission

To inspire and enable participation in sports and physical activities across all age groups, ensuring inclusivity, excellence, and opportunities for all.

Key Objectives

  • Develop infrastructure and facilities for sports at the grassroots and elite levels.
  • Organize national and international sports events to encourage participation.
  • Provide support and training for athletes to compete at the highest levels.
  • Collaborate with schools, colleges, and communities to promote physical education.

Our Achievements

Over the years, the Department of Sports has achieved remarkable milestones, including:

  • Establishing over 200 sports training centers nationwide.
  • Organizing international sports championships with over 50 participating countries.
  • Providing scholarships and funding for over 1,000 promising athletes annually.
