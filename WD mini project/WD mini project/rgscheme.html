Rajiv Gandhi Scheme

About the Scheme

The Rajiv Gandhi Khel Abhiyan (RGKA) was a central government scheme aimed at promoting sports in rural India by developing infrastructure like block-level sports complexes, organizing competitions, and providing training programs. It encouraged mass participation, inclusivity, and the development of traditional and modern sports, fostering a grassroots sporting culture. The scheme was later integrated into the Khelo India Programme for streamlined sports development.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official website of the Sports Department.
  2. Fill out the application form available under the Rajiv Gandhi Scheme section.
  3. Submit the necessary documents and wait for confirmation.
