Common Wealth Games 2022

The 2022 Commonwealth Games in Birmingham, England, saw India achieve a strong performance, securing a total of 61 medals: 22 gold, 16 silver, and 23 bronze. This impressive haul solidified India's position as a rising force in global sports.

Dominant Performances:


Wrestling:

The Indian wrestling team emerged as a powerhouse, capturing 6 gold medals. Ravi Dahiya, Bajrang Punia, and Sakshi Malik, all Olympic medalists, continued their winning streaks.


Table Tennis:

The Indian table tennis contingent proved its dominance, securing 4 gold medals. Achanta Sharath Kamal, Manika Batra, and Gnanasekaran Sathiyan led the charge with exceptional performances.


Boxing:

Indian boxers displayed their prowess, winning 3 gold medals. Nikhat Zareen, Lovlina Borgohain, and Amit Panghal emerged victorious in their respective weight categories.

Badminton:

The Indian badminton team, led by experienced players, secured 3 gold medals. Lakshya Sen, P.V. Sindhu, and the men's doubles pair of Satwiksairaj Rankireddy and Chirag Shetty contributed significantly to India's gold medal haul.

Weightlifting:

Indian weightlifters continued their impressive run, securing 3 gold medals. Mirabai Chanu, a renowned weightlifter, once again showcased her dominance with another gold medal.

Beyond the Medal Count:


Lawn Bowls:

India made history by winning its first-ever Commonwealth Games gold medal in Lawn Bowls, with the women's fours team securing the top spot.

Athletics:

India secured 8 medals in athletics, including a gold medal in the men's javelin throw by Neeraj Chopra.

Hockey:

The Indian men's hockey team secured a bronze medal, showcasing their consistent performance in the sport.

A New Era for Indian Sports:


The 2022 Commonwealth Games showcased the dedication and hard work of Indian athletes across various disciplines. The impressive medal tally not only boosted national pride but also inspired a new generation of athletes to pursue sporting excellence. India's consistent performance across different sports demonstrates the nation's growing sporting potential and its journey towards becoming a global sporting powerhouse.
