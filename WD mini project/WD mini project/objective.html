Objectives of Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports

Key Objectives
  • Promoting physical fitness, sports participation, and excellence among youth in India.
  • Fostering leadership skills and personality development through various youth programs and activities.
  • Encouraging youth involvement in social and national development through various platforms.
  • Creating opportunities for youth to actively participate in various sports and recreational activities.
  • Supporting the development of infrastructure and training for athletes in various sports disciplines.
  • Enhancing international cooperation in the field of sports and youth development.
  • Strengthening youth participation in decision-making processes, including youth empowerment and advocacy.
  • Promoting inclusion, equality, and opportunity for youth from all sections of society, especially marginalized communities.
